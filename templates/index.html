Information Page


With the best intentions.

With the best of intentions we created with App to improve the social aspect of MUNNY. We often feel that there is so much so do we wont get to meet all the wonderful, interesting and inspiring people of MUNNY. This app houses a range of features that we feel all support the MUNNY experience, please read the briefs below and play around for a bit.


Fruitful or not

Nobody really know why fruitful is such a frequent word in political speeches, but it is. We made a mini-game in this app such that you can bet on wether or not you think a speaker will say fruitful in their speech (or not). Tap the icons below to find it.


Support Tickets

We often find that people are running back and forth to and from ad-staff with requests and questions. With the new ticket system you can pass simple messages to ad-staff directly on your phone.


MUNNY Friend-Finder

The Friend-Finder system is truly a first for MUNNY. For all those awesome people you meet but never remember to add on facebook. We have the friend-finder tool for both native students and guests. It requires a verysimple facebook registration and then you just have to find and request a facebook page from the people you want to add. Once they request your's you will see each others pages as links on the page.


For those pesky bugs!

If you find a fault or error in the system (app). Please feel free to consult me (Benjamin Rasmussen) as i will fix it post-haste in such case. Otherwise have a great time at MUNNY!


Notice from party committee

The party comittee would like you, the social event participant, to bring cash in the form of preferably DKK. in order to make transactions at the events.
